Music News

Biggest Names In Music Today

Kendrick Lamar has been in the headlines recently following a back and forth diss battle between Drake and himself. His recent song "Like That" with Future and Metro Boomin takes some direct shots at Drake and his most recent album For All The Dogs.

Rex Orange County released a new album a few years ago and has been a presence in the bedroom pop industry since. He has also been finishing up his tour that he had stopped due to family reasons two years ago.

Wiz Khalifa performed at the NBA on TNT Live show during NBA All-Star Weekend. He dropped a new album recently and has been consistently in the spotlight for most of his career.

OneRepublic is coming off the back of a hit, "I Ain't Worried," from the summer blockbuster Top Gun: Maverick.
