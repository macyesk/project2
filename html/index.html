About Us

Conceived by a group of music aficionados with a penchant for puns, Four the Music-Heads began as a small record store with big dreams of spreading the joy of music to the masses.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to inspire a lifelong love affair with music, fostering discovery, connection, and a deep appreciation for the transformative power of sound.
